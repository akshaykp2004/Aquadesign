Our Process

A systematic approach to creating beautiful, thriving aquascapes from start to finish.

The Aquascaping Journey

From initial consultation to thriving ecosystem, we follow a proven 4-step methodology

1
Consult

Consultation & Planning

Week 1-2

We start with an in-depth consultation to understand your vision, space, budget, and goals. We assess your space dimensions, lighting, and technical requirements.

  • Discuss aquascaping style preferences
  • Measure space and evaluate feasibility
  • Assess existing equipment (if applicable)
  • Define budget and timeline
  • Create detailed project plan
2
Design

Design & Mockups

Week 2-4

Our designers create multiple mockup options reflecting different aquascaping styles. You'll receive detailed visual concepts with plant and hardscape specifications.

  • Create 2-3 design concepts
  • Provide detailed plant selections
  • Equipment recommendations
  • CO2 & lighting specifications
  • Unlimited revisions included
3
Setup

Professional Installation

Week 4-6

Our installation team brings your design to life. We handle all setup, equipment installation, landscaping, planting, and water preparation with expert precision.

  • Equipment assembly & testing
  • Hardscape arrangement
  • Substrate & plant installation
  • Water chemistry testing
  • Cycling & stabilization
4
Growth

Long-Term Growth & Support

Ongoing

Your journey doesn't end at installation. We provide ongoing maintenance, monitoring, and support to ensure your aquascape thrives for years to come.

  • Monthly or bi-weekly maintenance
  • Water parameter monitoring
  • Plant health optimization
  • Equipment servicing
  • Dashboard access & tracking

Our Core Methodology

We follow established best practices combined with innovative techniques

🎨

Artistic Design

We apply proven design principles like the Golden Ratio, color theory, and spatial composition to create visually stunning aquascapes.

🔬

Scientific Approach

Every design is grounded in aquatic science - water chemistry, bioload calculations, and ecological balance are paramount.

🌿

Natural Ecosystems

We design aquascapes that mimic natural habitats, promoting thriving flora, fauna, and long-term ecological stability.

🔄

Sustainable Practices

Eco-friendly approach with sustainable plant sourcing, minimal waste, and efficient resource management.

📚

Continuous Learning

Our team stays updated with latest aquascaping techniques, plant varieties, and technological innovations.

🌿

Client Collaboration

We work closely with clients throughout the entire process, ensuring your vision is perfectly realized.
