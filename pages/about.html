About AquaDesign

15 years of expertise in creating stunning aquascapes that transform spaces and bring joy to thousands of clients worldwide.

Our Legacy

A Passion for
Perfection

Founded in 2009, AquaDesign started with a simple passion: creating beautiful, thriving aquatic ecosystems. What began as a small aquascaping studio has grown into a leading design firm.

We combine artistic vision with scientific knowledge to create aquariums that are as healthy as they are beautiful. Today, we've designed over 500 aquascapes, each one a unique masterpiece.
