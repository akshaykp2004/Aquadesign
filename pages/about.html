About AquaDesign

15 years of expertise in creating stunning aquascapes that transform spaces and bring joy to thousands of clients worldwide.

Our Story

Founded in 2009, AquaDesign started with a simple passion: creating beautiful, thriving aquatic ecosystems. What began as a small aquascaping studio has grown into a leading design firm, trusted by homeowners, businesses, and interior designers worldwide.

Our journey has been defined by continuous learning, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. We combine artistic vision with scientific knowledge to create aquariums that are as healthy as they are beautiful.

Today, we've designed over 500 aquascapes, each one a unique masterpiece. From intimate nano tanks to spectacular 2000+ gallon installations, we bring the beauty of nature into every space we touch.
