Featured Project

Luxury Residential Installation

This stunning 200-gallon planted aquascape was designed for a high-end residential property in Miami. The design combines elements of Japanese aquascaping techniques with modern minimalism, featuring rare aquatic plants and a sophisticated lighting system.

The aquascape was custom-designed to complement the modern architecture of the home, with a natural wood frame and concealed filtration system. The project took 6 weeks from initial consultation to final installation.
