Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our luxury aquascaping services.

How long does a custom installation take?

+
Typically, a professional installation takes between 4 to 8 hours depending on the complexity of the design and the size of the aquarium. This includes hardscaping, planting, and initial water conditioning.

Do I need to be an expert to maintain my aquascape?

+
Not at all! We design our systems for various skill levels. Additionally, our comprehensive maintenance plans handle all the complex tasks like trimming, water chemistry, and equipment servicing for you.

What is the benefit of a professional design?

+
Professional design ensures long-term biological stability and aesthetic harmony. We use Golden Ratio principles and deep biological expertise to create an ecosystem that thrives rather than just survives.

Do you offer international consultations?

+
Yes, we provide virtual design consultations worldwide. For physical installations, we currently serve the greater metropolitan areas, but we can arrange travel for large-scale premium projects.
